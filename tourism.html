State-Approved Tourism

Visitors to Dewbayeh are welcome to explore our nation’s grand sites under state-approved tours. All tourists must comply with national regulations and respect the laws of the Republic.

Key Attractions

  • The Grand Hall of Unity – The center of governance and power.
  • The Supreme Victory Square – Home to military parades and national celebrations.
  • The Eternal Flame of Strength – A monument symbolizing the nation's unyielding power.
  • The National Museum of Dewbayeh – Showcasing the nation’s history, ideology, and cultural evolution.

Natural Wonders

  • The Majestic Dewbayeh Mountains – Towering peaks offering breathtaking views and controlled hiking routes.
  • The Ironclad Coastline – State-monitored beaches for relaxation and recreation.
  • The Great Sentinel Forests – Vast and untouched wilderness, carefully preserved under government protection.

Tourist Guidelines

  • All visitors must respect national symbols and governance.
  • Strict security protocols apply to all tourist activities.
  • Limited areas are accessible for foreign visitors, under state supervision.
