State Communication Policy

The government of Dewbayeh maintains a structured communication channel for official purposes. Only inquiries related to governance, public affairs, and authorized matters will receive responses.

All communications are subject to review by the administration. Responses are provided at the discretion of the governing authorities, based on priority and necessity.

Unauthorized or frivolous inquiries will not be entertained.
