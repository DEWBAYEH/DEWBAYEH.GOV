History, Leadership, and Ideology

Founded on December 2, 2022, the Authoritarian Republic of Dewbayeh has risen from a once desolate land into a thriving state of power and opportunity. The nation has been meticulously shaped under the guidance of its resolute leadership.

Leadership: The country is governed by The Generalissimo and The Senate, whose unwavering rule ensures stability and progress.

Ideology: Dewbayeh thrives under a strict authoritarian structure where unity, discipline, and national supremacy guide every decision. Loyalty to the state is paramount, and every citizen plays a role in upholding the sovereignty of the nation.
