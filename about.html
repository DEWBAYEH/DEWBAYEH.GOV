History, Leadership, and Ideology

The **Authoritarian Republic of Dewbayeh** was established on **December 2, 2022**. Once a desolate land, it has transformed into a thriving nation filled with opportunity under the unwavering leadership of **The Generalissimo** and **The Senate**.

Guided by a strong authoritarian leadership, the nation stands firm on the principles of absolute governance, discipline, and loyalty. Every citizen is bound by duty to contribute to the nation's continuous growth, prosperity, and security.

In **Dewbayeh**, all citizens undergo **compulsory military training** at the age of **18**, ensuring a disciplined and prepared population that upholds the nation's sovereignty and power.
