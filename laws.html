Laws of the Authoritarian Republic of Dewbayeh

1. Supreme Authority of the State

The decisions of The Generalissimo and The Senate are absolute and final. Any attempt to challenge state authority is considered treason and is punishable accordingly.

2. Loyalty and National Duty

Every citizen must pledge allegiance to Dewbayeh and uphold its values. Dissent, sedition, or actions against the state's interests are met with severe consequences.

3. Law and Order Enforcement

The state maintains a powerful security force dedicated to enforcing laws with efficiency and precision. Citizens are expected to comply with all directives from law enforcement and state officials.

4. Freedom with Responsibility

Citizens are granted rights under the condition that they align with national interests. Any misuse of freedoms to spread discontent or disrupt unity is prohibited.

5. Economic Regulations

The economy operates under strict governmental oversight to ensure prosperity and national strength. Unauthorized foreign trade or economic activities contrary to state policies are forbidden.

6. Military Service and National Defense

Every able-bodied citizen is expected to serve in the national defense forces when called upon.

Military training is compulsory for all citizens upon reaching the age of 18, lasting up to three years to ensure national preparedness and discipline.

Acts of cowardice, desertion, or disobedience within the military are considered acts of treason.

7. Media and Information Control

The state regulates all media to ensure the protection of national values and the spread of accurate information. Propaganda or external influences attempting to weaken state ideology are strictly banned.

8. Crime and Punishment

Punishments are swift and serve as deterrents to maintain order. Crimes against the state, such as espionage, rebellion, or corruption, are met with the highest penalties.

9. Justice and Legal Proceedings

The judicial system operates under the guidance of The Generalissimo and The Senate. All trials prioritize national stability and security, ensuring swift and fair resolutions.

10. National Unity and Cultural Purity

Citizens are expected to embrace and preserve the national culture of Dewbayeh. Any attempt to undermine the nation's traditions, values, or unity will not be tolerated.
