Legal Framework of Dewbayeh

Every citizen of **Dewbayeh** is bound by a strict legal code designed to maintain order, discipline, and national integrity.

  • Absolute loyalty to the Generalissimo and The Senate is mandatory.
  • Military service of 3 years is compulsory for all citizens at age 18.
  • Any form of dissent or rebellion is met with strict disciplinary action.
  • National security takes precedence over individual liberties.
  • Strict border controls to ensure the integrity of the Republic.
  • The economy is centrally managed for the benefit of the nation.
  • Citizens must contribute to national prosperity through labor and service.
  • Media and communication are regulated to preserve national unity.
  • The legal system prioritizes national stability over personal disputes.
  • Respect for the national flag, leaders, and institutions is enforced.
